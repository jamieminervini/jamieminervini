Dog Breeds M-Z

Maltese

The Maltese is small in size, weighing between 8 and 12 pounds. They typically live between 12 and 16 years. They are very playful, have a lot of energy, and are very affectionate. They are also very social and love to be around people.

Newfoundland

The Newfoundland is a giant dog, with the female weighing between 100 and 120 pounds and the male weighing 130 to 150 pounds. The “Newfie” is a gentle giant and is extremely sweet-tempered. They typically live for about 8 to 10 years.

Otterhound

The Otterhound originated in England and is medium to large in size. The male Otterhound can weigh between 90 and 110 pounds and the female can weigh between 64 and 90 pounds. They live for about 10 to 13 years and are amiable, even tempered, and boisterous.

Poodle

Poodles are very intelligent, faithful, and alert. They live for about 10-12 years and are usually small-medium in size, but can be bigger. There are Toy Poodles, Miniature Poodles, Standard Poodles, and Medium Poodles.

Rottweiler

A Rottweiler is usually large in size, weighing up to 130 pounds for males and 110 for females. They are self-assured, good-natured, and courages. They would be best suited for an experienced dog owner and live for about 8-10 years.

Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky is a medium sized working dog that originated in north-eastern Siberia, Russia. They are very outgoing, intelligent, and gentle. They love to have room to run and are very fast. They are best suited for experienced dog owners who have a lot of land. Siberian Huskies typically live for about 12 to 15 years.

Tibetan Mastiff

A Tibetan Mastiff is a very large breed of dog. The males can weigh up to 160 pounds and the females can weigh up to 120. They have a thick coat and enjoy the cold weather. Tibetan Mastiffs are stubborn, protective, and strong-willed. They were originally bred to protect sheep from wolves, bears, and other predators. They can live up to 15 years.

Vizsla

The Vizsla is medium sized dog and has a life span of about 12 to 15 years. Vizslas first originated in Hungary and are sporting dogs. They are very gentle, affectionate, and loyal. The Vizsla would be good for new dog owners.

Welsh Terrier

Welsh Terriers are hypoallergenic and are small-medium in size, weighing up to 20 pounds. They are very intelligent, friendly, and alert. If someone in your family has a dog allergy, a Welsh Terrier would be a good dog to consider adopting. They would also be good for new dog owners.

Xoloitzcuintli

The Xoloitzcuintli, or Mexican Hairless Dog, is an extremely small hairless dog that lives for 12 to 15 years. When fully grown, they weigh between 4.5 and 8.5 pounds. Because they are hairless, they are hypoallergenic. The Xoloitzcuintli would be good for new dog owners because they are intelligent, companionable, and calm.

Yorkshire Terrier

The Yorkshire Terrier is extremely small, weighing about 6-8 pounds when fully grown. They are hypoallergenic and live about 13-16 years. They would be good for first-time dog owners and are intelligent, confident, and independent.
