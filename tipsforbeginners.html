Tips for Beginners

Grooming

How much fur and slobber can you tolerate? If you can't tolerate a bit of mess, a dog that doesnt shed or drool so much would be right for you. Labradoodles, Malteses, and Yorkies are just a few of the breeds that do not shed or drool. Some breeds that might not be good for you are St. Bernards, Bernese Mountain Dogs, or Bloodhounds.

Behavior

Because of the high-stress environment, some dogs tend to act differently in a shelter than they would at home. That being said, they might act more agressive or shy than they normally would in a home. However, it is important to observe how your potential dog acts around other dogs and people.

Adopt!

Always adopt from a shelter. Although it can be hard to find the right dog at a shelter, there are poeple who work there that are trained to help people find the right dog. Use that free service.
