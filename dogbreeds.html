Dog Breeds A-L

Akita

Native to Japan, the Akita is medium in size and has a lifespan of about 10-15 years. Akitas are typically good for experienced dog owners and are extremely affectionate towards family. However, if they are not socialized properly in their early life, they can show aggression towards strangers and unfamiliar dogs.

Bernese Mountain Dog

Bernese Mountain Dogs are large in size, live 7-10 years and can weigh up to 110 pounds. They are extremely loyal and are very dependent on their owners. They are also mostly friendly with strangers if they are properly socialized as a puppy.

Collie

Collies originated in Scotland and have a lifespan of about 10-14 years. They are medium-large in size, weighing between 50 and 70 pounds once full grown. Collies are great for first-time dog owners. They are extremely loyal and very smart which makes them easy to train.

Dalmatian

Dalmatians live for about 10-13 years and would make excellent watchdogs. They are not typically aggressive towards strangers, but they are always alert about their surroundings and don't tend to bark for no reason. If something isn't right, your Dalmatian will let you know.

English Foxhound

The English Foxhound is medium in size, can weigh up to 70 pounds and live about 10-13 years. They need a lot of exercise and are extremely dog friendly. They are very patient and would be good for a family with children. English Foxhounds have a thick, dense coat and shed a moderate amount. They are relatively easy to groom because they only need to be brushed once a week and bathed as needed.

French Bulldog

The French Bulldog lives up to 13 years and weighs no more than 28 pounds. Because they are extremely small and are extremely friendly with strangers, they are great for people living in apartments. They have high social needs, but are not very patient so a family with kids probably would not be the best home for a French Bulldog.

Golden Retreiver

Golden Retrievers are a very gentle and intelligent breed. They are typically great with kids and strangers and are social with other dogs as well. Because they love people so much, they would not make good guard dogs. They would be a great dog for first-time dog owners.

Havanese

The Havanese is very small in size, weighing anywhere from 10 to 15 pounds when fully grown. They usually have hair and not fur which makes them hypoallergenic. They love attention and are very playful, intelligent, and responsive. They would make a good first dog for inexperienced dog owners.

Irish Setter

The Irish setter is a medium size breed of dog. Females can weigh up to 64 pounds and males can weigh up to 70. They live for about 12-15 years and are not hypoallergenic. The Irish Setter is very playful, compassionate, and independent. They would probably be better for more experienced dog owners.

Jack Russell Terrier

Jack Russell Terriers are a small in size and live for about 13-16 years. They can weigh up to 18 pounds when fully grown. They are very quick, agile, and playful. They are also fearless, stubborn, and intelligent. Jack Russell Terriers would be better suited for a highly experienced dog owner.

Karelian Bear Dog

The Karelian Bear Dog is medium in size, weighting up to 50 pounds once full grown. They love for about 11-13 years and are not hypoallergenic. Karelian Bear Dogs are very tenacious, cautious, loyal, and brave. They would probably be good for a dog owner with little to no experience.

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dogs in the UK and US. They live anywhere between 10 and 14 years and are large in size. They are very friendly, loyal, intelligent, and gentle. A Labrador Retriever would be perfect for a new dog owner
